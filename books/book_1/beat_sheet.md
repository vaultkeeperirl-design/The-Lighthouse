# Book 1: The Sentinel's Grift - Beat Sheet

## Act 1: The Inheritance & The Invitation
### Chapter 1: The Eviction
- **Scene 1**: Eddie’s "office" in the shipping container. The heat, the meat pie, the unpaid site fees.
- **Scene 2**: The confrontation with Barry. Eddie’s failed attempts at legal jargon and bribes.
- **Scene 3**: The lawyer (Henderson) arrives. The reveal of Uncle Bart’s death and the inheritance.
- **Scene 4**: Eddie’s "grand exit" in the Holden Commodore.

### Chapter 2: The Gateway to Nowhere
- **Scene 1**: The drive to Port Whisper. Eddie’s internal monologue about becoming a "Lighthouse King."
- **Scene 2**: Arrival at *The Sinking Anchor*. Eddie tries to establish dominance and fails.
- **Scene 3**: Meeting the locals (Groggy). The warning about Uncle Bart and the "weirdness."
- **Scene 4**: The midnight crossing of the bridge. Atmospheric setup of the Sentinel.

### Chapter 3: The Sentinel
- **Scene 1**: Entering the lighthouse. The smell of jasmine and salt.
- **Scene 2**: Discovering the elevator. The brass filigree and the strange control panel.
- **Scene 3**: The first "mishap." Eddie presses the '0' (Mouth) button.
- **Scene 4**: The Glass Forest. Meeting the three-eyed Crow. Eddie’s panic-driven button mashing.

### Chapter 4: The Interdimensional Commute
- **Scene 1**: The elevator zips through several realms (The Void, The Clockwork City, etc.) as Eddie panics.
- **Scene 2**: Eddie manages to return to the "Ground Floor" (Port Whisper). He’s shaken but immediately starts thinking of how to charge people for the ride.
- **Scene 3**: Eddie tries to clean up the lighthouse to make it look like a PI office. He finds Bart’s old uniform—a lighthouse keeper’s coat that’s way too big for him.
- **Scene 4**: Eddie "hires" his first employee: **Baz**, a local who was kicked out of the pub for trying to sell "pre-loved" bait.

### Chapter 5: The Client
- **Scene 1**: **Sarah** arrives at the lighthouse. She’s frantic. Her dog, Bluey, vanished from her backyard (which borders the lighthouse stack).
- **Scene 2**: Eddie performs a "Standard Intake Interview" which involves him asking completely irrelevant questions (e.g., "On a scale of 1 to 10, how much does the dog know about my debts?").
- **Scene 3**: Eddie accepts the case for $50 and a promise of a meat pie. He finds a strange tuft of blue fur that glows in the dark.
- **Scene 4**: Sarah reveals that she isn't just looking for the dog—Bluey was wearing a collar with a "special key" that her father left her.

## Act 2: The Investigation
### Chapter 6: The Grift Begins
- **Scene 1**: Eddie and Baz "investigate" the backyard. Eddie tries to use a metal detector he found in the basement, but it only picks up "ghost signals."
- **Scene 2**: Baz reveals he knows about the elevator. His grandfather told him stories about "The Border Guard."
- **Scene 3**: Eddie decides they need to "interrogate" the town. They head back to the pub.
- **Scene 4**: The "Interrogation": Eddie tries to act like a hard-boiled detective and gets laughed at. He accidentally discovers that Sarah’s father was Uncle Bart’s only friend.

### Chapter 7: The Blue Clue
- **Scene 1**: Back at the lighthouse, Eddie finds the glow-in-the-dark fur is reacting to the elevator.
- **Scene 2**: The elevator opens on its own. Inside is a single item: A silver whistle that sounds like a human scream.
- **Scene 3**: Eddie blows the whistle. A shadow-version of Bluey appears for a split second, then runs into the elevator.
- **Scene 4**: Eddie and Baz chase the shadow into the elevator. Eddie presses the "Spiral" button.

### Chapter 8: The Noir Realm
- **Scene 1**: They arrive in a world that is literally black and white. Everything is high-contrast, and jazz music plays from nowhere.
- **Scene 2**: Eddie loves it. He immediately starts narrating his own life in a gravelly voice. Baz is terrified.
- **Scene 3**: They find a "Bar" in the Noir Realm. The bartender is a sentient shadow.
- **Scene 4**: The Shadow Bartender gives them a clue: "The dog is with the Collector."

### Chapter 9: Big Vinnie’s Arrival
- **Scene 1**: Back in the real world, **Big Vinnie** (the loan shark) pulls up to Port Whisper in a black SUV.
- **Scene 2**: Vinnie starts asking around for "The Piece of Shit in the suit."
- **Scene 3**: Vinnie meets Sarah at the general store. He realizes Sarah is the "Key" to getting the lighthouse's "treasure."
- **Scene 4**: Vinnie heads for the bridge at low tide.

## Act 3: Escalation
### Chapter 10: The Siege of the Sentinel
- **Scene 1**: Eddie and Baz are trying to monetize the Noir Realm (Eddie wants to sell "Noir Photoshoots").
- **Scene 2**: They see Vinnie’s SUV crossing the bridge. Eddie panics and tries to barricade the door.
- **Scene 3**: Vinnie breaks in. He doesn't want money—he knows about the "Gate" (the elevator). He’s been working for a "silent partner" who wants to control the realms.
- **Scene 4**: The elevator chime rings. Something *else* starts trying to get in from the other side.
