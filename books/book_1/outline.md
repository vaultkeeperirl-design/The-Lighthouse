# Book 1: The Sentinel's Grift - Expanded Outline

**Target Length:** 50,000+ words
**Series Tone:** Always Sunny meets Twin Peaks meets Gravity Falls.

## Act 1: The Inheritance & The Invitation
- **Chapter 1: The Eviction.** Eddie loses his shipping container office and inherits the Sentinel.
- **Chapter 2: The Gateway to Nowhere.** Eddie arrives in Port Whisper and fails to impress the locals.
- **Chapter 3: The Sentinel.** Eddie enters the lighthouse and discovers the interdimensional elevator.
- **Chapter 4: The Glass Forest.** Eddie’s first trip to another realm. He meets the Three-Eyed Crow and panics.
- **Chapter 5: The Sidekick.** Eddie recruits Baz to help him "monetize" the lighthouse.

## Act 2: The Case of the Vanishing Kelpie
- **Chapter 6: The Client.** Sarah Jenkins hires Eddie to find her dog, Bluey, and the key on his collar.
- **Chapter 7: The Interrogation.** Eddie and Baz try to "work the town" for clues. They find the charred pawprint.
- **Chapter 8: The Screaming Whistle.** Eddie finds a strange artifact in the elevator that reveals the "Echo" of the dog.
- **Chapter 9: The Noir Realm.** Eddie and Baz enter the Spiral floor. Eddie becomes a hard-boiled narrator. They find a lead on "The Collector."
- **Chapter 10: The Debt Collector.** Big Vinnie arrives in Port Whisper, looking for Eddie and the "Gate."

## Act 3: The Gathering Storm
- **Chapter 11: The Scheme.** Eddie tries to trick Vinnie into entering the "Void" floor, but the plan backfires when the elevator malfunctions.
- **Chapter 12: The Collector’s Tax.** We meet the Collector—a being that wants the key around Bluey’s neck.
- **Chapter 13: Sarah’s Secret.** Eddie discovers that Sarah’s father and Uncle Bart were part of a secret society called "The Anchorites."
- **Chapter 14: The Buffer Realm.** To escape Vinnie, the gang hides in the "Toast" floor—a safe house that looks like a 70s kitchen.
- **Chapter 15: The Ticking City.** Baz gets lost in the Clockwork City. Eddie has to go on a "solo mission" (i.e., stumbling through a machine-world) to save him.

## Act 4: The Breach
- **Chapter 16: The Return of the Dog.** They find Bluey in the Echo Realm, but he’s "changed." He’s now three feet taller and speaks in riddles.
- **Chapter 17: Vinnie’s Partner.** The reveal of the "Silent Partner"—a local Port Whisper resident who wants to use the elevator to bring back the "Thirteenth Apostle."
- **Chapter 18: The Battle of the Bridge.** The storm hits. The bridge is the only thing keeping the Echo Realm from bleeding into Port Whisper.
- **Chapter 19: The Grift of a Lifetime.** Eddie realizes he can’t win a fight, so he has to "con" the entities. He stages a "fake auction" for the key.
- **Chapter 20: The New Guard.** Eddie saves the day (mostly by accident). Vinnie is sent to the Noir Realm to serve as a perpetual "Goop" in a detective story.
- **Chapter 21: Epilogue.** Eddie sets up "Holloway Investigations: Interdimensional Experts." He realizes he’s now the Border Guard, which means he has to actually do work. He hates it.
